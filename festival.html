FESTIVALS IN INDIA

  • Diwali is a festival of lights,celebrated every year in autumn.It is a two day festival that symbolizes the triumph of good over evil.

  • Holi is a festival of colours,celebrated every year in february or march.It is a two day festival that symbolizes the triumph of good over evil, the arrival of spring at the end of winter

  • Onam is a festival which is mainly in Kerala,celebrated every year in august or september.It is a ten day festival that symbolizes the homecoming of the mythical king Mahabali
